About

A girl who is ripped from her reality and dropped into a dream world. The Dreamer's Princess is a Maze Exploring, Bullet Hell, RPG currently in development for Android, iOS, and the Nintendo 3DS.

Features
  • Randomly Generated Levels where the player must adapt to a play style.
  • Different endings based on multiple factors, not just player choices.
  • Unexpected Mini bosses
  • Surprises waiting on every corner.
  • 10 Major areas, with 5 main chapters
